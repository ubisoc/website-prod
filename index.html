Our campaigns

Throughout the year we run campaigns for students on campus.

Freshers Week

Freshers Week is essentially Welcome Week at the University of Birmingham for new students. Students are introduced to the University with induction lectures and all Student Societies run a variety of events during Freshers Week to enable the new students to meet like minded people and make new friends.

  • More...

Charity Week

Charity Week is an annual campaign run by Islamic Societies across the country where they raise money for a particular cause over the course of a week.

  • More...

Discover Islam Week

Discover Islam Week is an annual campaign run by many Islamic Societies across the country to spread the message of Islam to everyone on campus over the course of a week.
