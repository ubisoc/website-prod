Prayer room facilities

The Guild of Students

Facilities (brothers and sisters side)

  • Qurans
  • Islamic books
  • Microwave
  • Kettle
  • Book rest desks

Directions/locations

When walking towards the guild from the clock tower, take the entrance that is next to the atm machine on your right. Walk straight on and around the pillar in the centre of the corridor and take the stairs up to the next floor. Sisters take the left through the double doors and brothers take a right and walk around until they come to the room with a shoe rack outside, that is the entrance to the prayer room for brothers. For the nearest toilets/wudhu facilities, follow the signs or ask a brother/sister inside the prayer room.

Sisters' side

Brothers' side

Chaplaincy

Facilities

  • Qurans
  • Prayer mats
  • Chairs and tables

Directions/location

Take the main entrance and then go through the brown double doors to your left that are next to the reception. For toilets/wudhu facilities take the stairs going downstairs and then take a right at the bottom of the stairs and follow the signs.

Entrance

For jumuah, sisters take the entrance on the side of the chaplaincy and brothers take the main entrance. Outside jumuah times, the entrance is the same.

NOTE: The facility may not always be available due to other events occurring within the room.


Chaplaincy

Medical School Library - Room CLG30

Facilities

  • Brothers and sisters section (separated in 2 sides by large curtains, entrance for both is the same) however facilities separate for both.
  • Prayer mats stored in a chest of draws
  • Islamic books e.g. copies of the Quran (including English translated versions), Islamic educational books, Islamic history books etc...
  • CDs with list of different duas
  • Chairs

Directions/location

Located on the lower ground floor in Room CLG30. From the ground floor, take the stairs downstairs (or use lift option '-1') then take left through double doors. Then turn left and follow around. You will see a sign directing towards the prayer room. Follow sign until you come to the room as shown in the picture below. For wudhu, continue walking past the prayer room and take first right, female toilets will then be to your left and male toilets to your right.

Medical School

Mech Eng room S10B (brothers only)

Facilities

  • Small table and a couple of chairs
  • Prayer mats (already laid out)

Direction/location

If you are coming from Selly Oak then take the first entrance into the mechanical engineering building. Upon entering take the stairs to go up on your right and then go straight until you come to a corridor on your left with a sign above which says 'Advanced machining/manufacturing processes lab sign'. There are stairs going up, go up the stairs (for toilets go downstairs as shown by sign directions). Keep going until you get to the 2nd floor.

On the 2nd floor, go through the brown double doors to your left then go straight and take a right. Follow the corridor around until you get to room S10B (it is at the very end).

Mech Eng Room S10B

Chemical Engineering

Facilities

  • Prayer mat/carpet
  • 1 chair

Direction locations

Upon entering the chemical engineering building through the front entrance take a right and go through the blue double doors, then walk straight around the corridor until you get to stairs that are going up. Next to the stairs there is an empty space. Go there and under the stairs. There is a chair with a prayer mat on it.

For the nearest toilets follow the directions that are hanging around the walls and ceiling within the building.

Chemical Engineering

Computer Science

Facilities

  • Prayer mats
  • 1 chair
  • For both brothers and sisters

Directions/location

Upon entering the computer science building, take a right and go through the brown double doors which have a sign for the 'Stairs' above them. Walk up the stairs until you get to the top floor. On your right there will be a sign about the prayer mats and below the sign are the prayers mats and signs. For the nearest toilets follow the signs.

Computer Science

Gisbert Kapp

Facilities

  • None

Directions/location

From the main entrance, walk around until you get to the reception. Walk through the glass double doors next to the reception desk (as shown in the 1st image). When you walk through there are some stairs going down and to the side there is a little corridor leading to a lift entrance. This area is very rarely used, therefore you should be able to pray here without any issues. It is also out of the way. Use your coat or some other piece of clothing as a prayer mat if you do not have your own and pray facing in the direction show in the last image. For the nearest toilets, follow the signs or ask at reception. Toilets are located next to the café on the ground floor. This is a good area to use if you have lectures in gisbert kapp.
