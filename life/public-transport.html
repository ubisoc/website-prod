Public transport

The University of Birmingham has great transport links to help you get to the places you want to be in Birmingham.

Train

The University is the only University in the UK with a train station campus and trains from here reach Birmingham New Street (City Centre) in 7 minutes (going through Five Ways).

Bus

The best buses to take you to the City Centre are the 61/63 buses. There are mutliple bus stops on Bristol road which you can take which all take you to the City Centre in around 20/25 minutes.

Fortunately, our Guild of Students has worked hard to lobby the council to give students cheaper bus fares so now, starting from the academic year 2017/18, every student can take a bus to the City Centre for only £1!

Normal fares are £4 for a daysaver (unlimited travel on any bus for the whole Birmingham for the whole day).
