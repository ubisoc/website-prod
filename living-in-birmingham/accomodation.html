Accomodation

1st Year Students

Generally, for first year undergraduate students, the University offers halls. Most students normally choose this as their default option.

If you would like, you can try and find Muslim house mates using our social media (Facebook, WhatsApp etc) to get a few people together and rent a house/accommodation privately.

Everyone else

Most students who are not 1st Year Students generally rent houses privately with a few friends or rent one of the 3rd party halls.

If you are looking for accommodation or Muslim house mates, you can join our accommodation Facebook groups:

  • Brothers - https://www.facebook.com/groups/811677035581551
  • Sisters - https://www.facebook.com/groups/408326482702433

Or you can email:
