Discover Islam Week 2018

In the name of Allah, the Most Beneficient, the Most Merciful, we begin and end.

Discover Islam Week has come, a week of dialogue, a week of love, a week of kindness, a week of gratitude, a week of brotherhood and sisterhood, a special week, let's make the most out it before it's gone.

What is Discover Islam Week?

This year's Discover Islam Week will take place from the 5th of February to the 9th of February.

The week consists of a variety of talks and events, and various socials which aims are: to introduce, to further understandings of and to encourage discussion of Islam in times where scarcity of dialogue and the shifting of focus to the narrative of various media outlets created a deteriorating effect in society's understanding of Islam.

Each day of the week will feature programs that explore the Muslim experience, from lectures to discussions from panels to Q&As. Each talk will cover a specific aspect of Islam and the impact of it in the society. Discover Islam Week is thus an amazing opportunity for all who are interested in Islam and want to explore topics such as 'Women in Islam' or 'Jihad' and many more. The objective of the week is therefore to create an atmosphere of mutual understanding which will help to foster dialogue between people.
